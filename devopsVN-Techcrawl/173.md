# Quan Huynh

![image](https://images.spr.so/cdn-cgi/imagedelivery/j42No7y-dcokJuNgXeA0ig/9da9898d-47da-4b1c-adbe-3076e37d06dd/Untitled/w=1920,quality=90,fit=scale-down)

I am a DevOps Lead at FinX, where I design, build, and operate mission-critical infrastructure for digital banking products on Amazon Web Services (AWS). My career has been rooted in the financial sector — an environment where security, resilience, and scalability are non-negotiable — and that discipline shapes everything I build.

Beyond my day-to-day work, I create open-source tools and write for the broader engineering community. I am the author and maintainer of two open-source projects.

### Versus Incident

Open-source incident management tool with an AI SRE agent that watches your logs, learns normal patterns, and auto-creates incidents — plus webhook alerts, multi-channel notifications, and on-call escalation.

⇒ Check here: [![Embed GitHub](/images/embeds/github.png)Embed GitHub](https://github.com/VersusControl/versus-incident)

### DevOps AI Guidelines & Learning Path

First AI Journey for DevOps - with comprehensive learning paths, practical tips, and enterprise guidelines.

This repository provides everything you need to master AI in DevOps - from your first AI tool to becoming an AI Infrastructure Architect. Whether you're starting your AI journey, implementing AI in your team, or leading enterprise AI adoption, we've got comprehensive resources and proven frameworks to guide your success.

⇒ Check here: [![Embed GitHub](/images/embeds/github.png)Embed GitHub](https://github.com/VersusControl/devops-ai-guidelines)
