Intro 

For Technology Partners

 DevOps VietNam helps technology companies reach Vietnam’s DevOps, SRE, Cloud, infrastructure, and engineering communities through trusted content, campaigns, and community engagement.
 

 Built for DevTools, Cloud, SaaS, Security, Observability, AI Infrastructure, technical education companies, and hiring teams looking to engage Vietnam’s engineering audience.
 

 Partners 

Partner Work

 Technology partners work with DevOps VietNam to reach, educate, and engage Vietnam’s engineering community.
 
Featured 2026

 Goals 

When Partners Work With Us

 A trusted gateway to Vietnam’s engineering community.
 


Launch a Technical Campaign
Introduce products, features, or technical use cases to the right engineering audience.


Drive Webinar Registrations
Attract the right audience for webinars, workshops, product demos, and technical sessions.


Build Technical Trust
Build credibility through technical content, case studies, and community engagement.


Reach Engineering Talent
Bring your employer brand and open roles to DevOps, SRE, Cloud, and Platform Engineering talent.



 Form 

Partner Deck

 Request DevOps VietNam’s partner deck and engagement options.
 

 A concise overview of DevOps VietNam’s community reach, partnership formats, and selected case studies for technology partners.
 


What’s inside the deck?

The engineering audiences DevOps VietNam can help you reach.
Partnership formats designed for technology companies.
Case studies with Datadog, Linux Foundation | Education, and DataOnline.
Campaign recommendations based on your specific business objectives.




DevOps VietNam will use this information to share the deck and follow up when there is a relevant partnership fit.
