4. Logo Usage

## Logo Usage

The DevOps VietNam logo is available in approved versions for consistent use across different backgrounds and layouts.

Item 1: Do (Clear Space)

![Clear space](https://devops.vn/resources/assets/brands/logos/png/dovn_logo_h_rgb.png)

Keep the logo proportions and clear space intact.

Item 2: Do (Black or White)

![Black](https://devops.vn/resources/assets/brands/logos/png/dovn_logo_h_white.png)

Use the approved black or white logo versions.

Item 3: Do (Approved logomark)

![Logomark](https://devops.vn/resources/assets/brands/logos/png/dovn_logo_v_white.png)

Use the approved logomark only.

5. What to Avoid

## What to Avoid

To preserve the integrity of the logo, do not modify or distort it in the following ways.

Item 1: Don't (Stretch)

![Stretch](https://devops.vn/resources/assets/brands/logos/png/dovn_logo_v_rgb.png)

Full Red X SVG Overlay

Do not stretch, crop, or distort the logo.

Item 2: Don't (Inside shapes)

![Shape](https://devops.vn/resources/assets/brands/logos/png/dovn_logo_v_rgb.png)

Full Red X SVG Overlay

Do not place the logo inside custom shapes.

Item 3: Don't (Change colors)

![Color](https://devops.vn/resources/assets/brands/logos/png/dovn_logo_v_rgb.png)

Full Red X SVG Overlay

Do not change the logo colors.

Item 4: Don't (Effects)

![Effects](https://devops.vn/resources/assets/brands/logos/png/dovn_logo_v_rgb.png)

Full Red X SVG Overlay

Do not add effects such as drop shadows or 3D styling.

Item 5: Don't (Outline icon)

![Outline](https://devops.vn/resources/assets/brands/logos/png/dovn_logo_v_rgb.png)

Full Red X SVG Overlay

Do not use the icon as an outline-only mark.

Item 6: Don't (Alter framing)

![Framing](https://devops.vn/resources/assets/brands/logos/png/dovn_logo_v_rgb.png)

Full Red X SVG Overlay

Do not alter the icon proportions or framing.
